Who Am I?

Hello, I'm Ethan Varghese, a passionate and ambitious sophomore at Foothill High School. The world of coding, algorithms, and AI has captured my imagination and I've been spending countless hours exploring these fields, engrossed in the complexity and challenge they present. The soft glow of my computer screen at night often envelops me as I lose track of time, immersed in my latest coding project. When I'm not coding, I like to stay active and engaged in other activities. On the tennis court, I can feel the warmth of the sun on my skin and hear the satisfying sound of the ball hitting the racket strings. Playing the piano provides a different kind of sensory experience, as I can feel the smooth keys beneath my fingertips and lose myself in the delicate notes and melodies. Exploring these passions brings balance to my life, allowing me to express myself in different ways and stay engaged in the world around me. I'm grateful for the opportunities I've had so far to pursue these interests, and I look forward to discovering where my passions will take me next.
